Iced coffee

What's better than a coffee,It's an iced coffee duh!

Perfect for summer and winter.you can drink it anytime you want easy to make and tastes like heaven.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp of instant coffee powder
  • 2 tsp of sugar/any sweetener of your choice
  • 1/4 cup hot water
  • 500 ml full fat milk/milk substitutes
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Mix coffee powder and sugar in a glass.Pour hot water and mix until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Fill the glass with Ice.
  3. Pour milk in the glass.
  4. Stir it well and your iced coffee is ready.
