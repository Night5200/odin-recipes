Lemon Iced Tea

Tea and lemon a classic combination that is just amazing.

Referesh yourselves with Lemon iced tea.Easy to make takes little to no time and takes you on a fun ride.

Ingredients

  • 1 tea bag/1-1.5 tsp of tea leaves.
  • 2-2.5 tsp of sugar/honey
  • 400 ml Hot water
  • 30ml/2tbsp Lemon juice/
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add sugar/honey in 400 ml hot water.
  2. Add you tea bag/tea leaves and cover it for 5 min.(If using tea leaves boil it once on the stove.)
  3. After 5 min put it in a heat proof pitcher and let it cool for atleast 1 hour.
  4. Once it is cooled add lemon juice and give it a good stir.
  5. Serve in a glass full of ice and your lemon iced tea is ready.
