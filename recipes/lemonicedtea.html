Lemon Iced Tea

Tea and lemon a classic combination that is just amazing.

Referesh yourselves with Lemon iced tea.Easy to make takes little to no time and takes you on a fun ride.

Ingredients

  • 1 tea bag/1-1.5 tsp of tea.
  • 2-2.5 tsp of sugar/honey
  • 400 ml Hot water
  • 30ml/2tbsp Lemon juice/
  • Ice cubes
