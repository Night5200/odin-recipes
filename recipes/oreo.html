Oreo Milkshake

When life gives you oreos,You make oreo milkshake.

Ingredients

  • 7-8 oreos
  • 500 ml chilled whole milk
  • Choclate syrup
  • 2 scoops Vanilla Ice cream
  • Sugar/condensed milk

Instructions

  1. Add oreos,Milk,1 scoop of vanilla ice cream,sugar and condensed milk ina blender.
  2. Blend it on lowest speed for 10 seconds,and highest speed for 20 seconds to get a thick consistency.
  3. swirl some choclate syrup inside a glass.transfer it in that glass.
  4. Top it off with a scoop of vanilla ice cream,choclate syrup and a crushed oreo.
  5. Your milkshake is ready.
